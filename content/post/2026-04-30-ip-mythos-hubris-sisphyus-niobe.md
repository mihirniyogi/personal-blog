### what's this series about?

I've recently gotten into Greek Mythology and am now in the midst of Mythos by Stephen Fry. Rather than a contiguous flow of story, Mythos is a collection of short, distinct (but somehow related) stories featuring the birth and initial stories of Gods, Demigods and Mortals alike. I keep finding myself forgetting the stories, however. There are simply too many! Too many names, too many gods and too many shenanigans by Zeus. Thus, after each story or chapter, I aim to write up a short recap/review of my thoughts on the chapter. The hope is, after thinking about the chapter and seeing how it relates to my life or life in general, it would help me at least keep the lessons in mind and also make it easier to remember the stories themselves.
