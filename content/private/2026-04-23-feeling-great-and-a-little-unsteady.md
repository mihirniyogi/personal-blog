I'm feeling great this morning, despite doomscrolling for more than an hour. As is the usual with my feelings, I have no idea why I'm feeling great. Perhaps it's the fact that yesterday's trivia night went well. I made a few acquaintances (friends is pushing it). I did have fun though. If I do write about trivia night in detail, I'll put it in the public-facing blog. This private journal here is more to process my feelings, good or bad they may be.

Yea that's it really. I'm feeling great this Thursday morning. Perhaps it's because of the events that I'm looking forward to - brunch with Ben, an easy tutorial class later in the evening. Or maybe because these events are "known quantities" and there aren't any anxieties I could face. Case-in-point: in the moments before trivia night, I was scared out of my mind just because it's a new experience I haven't gone through before, with new people I haven't met before. 

And finally, why I'm writing about the "+" / good feelings in the first place, is because I think it's as important to understand the roots of these plus feelings as it is to understand those of the minus ones. And why is that? Perhaps I can _cook up_ a good day if I know and prepare the ingredients a priori. 

Going back to the "known quantities" I'm going to be facing today, it's interesting that meeting up with Ben doesn't cause me any stress or pressure; there's no fear of being late, or fear of making him wait. I'm not advance planning too much, just going with the flow. Just thought I would verbalise this...this reason for _not_ feeling anxious or fearful.
